About NetPrep

Your complete UGC NET IT preparation platform

NetPrep is a comprehensive platform designed to help aspirants prepare for the UGC NET IT & Computer Science examination. It covers the complete syllabus for Paper 1 and Paper 2 with detailed study material, mock exams and progress tracking.

Features

  • Comprehensive topic explanations with Markdown rendering
  • Timed mock exam generator with randomized questions
  • Detailed answer review with explanations
  • Progress tracking by subject and paper
  • Study timer and personal notes per topic
  • Score analytics and study activity charts
